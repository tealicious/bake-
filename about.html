BAKE! Has Arrived

Established by chef and founder Francois Picard in 2004 with the opening of Bake! cafe, Bake has grown to include restaurants in Bend Oregon, and Asheville North Carolina. Bake has a total of thee cafes established by award-winning pastry chef Kara Koehmstedt with multiple locations throughout Oregon, North Carolina and Colorado. Kara has also established a medical dispensary in her home city of Bend. The restaurants have gained world-wide recognition for their innovative take on edible goods, while supporting local, sustainable and responsible farmers and food purveyors. Bake! is also composed of Hydro Monthly magazine along with a new greenhouse dispensary called Tantalus Labs.
