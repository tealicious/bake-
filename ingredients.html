The Grow

Bake takes our ingredients seriously. We source only the highest quality and organically grown produce, starting with our cannabis. That means no chemically altered crops.


We offer three strands, a house sativa, indica, and hybrid, and can accommodate specific requests for catering orders.


In order to ensure consistency and a standard acceptable to our needs, Bake has partnered with the best commercial grow operation around. Visit their site to learn more about the process behind your favourite dessert's key ingredient.

The Dairy

Organic and free-range milk and eggs are the foundation food ingredients for everything we make, from our Organic, Cream-Top Milk to our European-Style, Organic Butter.


Animal care is the cornerstone of our vendor's farm operations. We strongly believe that the better their animals are treated, the healthier they are. And healthy, well-nourished cows and chickens produce better-quality, steroid-free, organic milk and eggs.

The Sweet Stuff

Bake buys it's sugars and other sweeteners from the only organic sugar supplier in the United States.


Florida Crystals supplies Bake with its various sweeteners and also brags a certified carbonfree® endorsement from carbonfund.org.
